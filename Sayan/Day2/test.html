About SAITM St. Andrew Institute of Technology and Management (SAITM) is located in Gurgaon, Haryana, India. The institute is affiliated with Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU), Rohtak, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The St. Andrews Institute of Technology & Management is situated in the industrial hub of Gurgaon, Delhi (NCR), India. SAITM Gurgaon benefits from its strategic location and accessibility.
